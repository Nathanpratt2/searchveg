❓Why use this site?

Finding a good vegan recipe shouldn't be hard. While modern search engines and AI tools have plenty of capabilities, this tool makes it easy to browse and filter recipes in a beautiful way, which you can count on being 100% vegan and straight from the creator themself.

SearchVeg is different. We have more vegan recipes in one place than anywhere on the Internet from over 100 of the world's best plant-based creators. New recipes are added every day. We also give you tons of helpful filters to find exactly what you need — whether that's a High Protein dinner, a Gluten-Free dessert, or a Budget-Friendly meal.

📈 Support the Creators

This platform acts as a gateway to the vegan creators. When you click a recipe, you are taken directly to the creator's website. We believe in supporting the bloggers who spend hours testing, photographing, and writing about their delicious meals.

✨ Features

  • Smart Filters: Sort by cuisine, mood, or diet type. Note: Nut and soy free are intended as preferences only, not medical allergen filters;
  • Strict Search: Type a blogger's name (like "Pick Up Limes") to see only their recipes.
  • Saved Recipes: Heart your favorites to build your own personal cookbook.
  • Dark Mode: Easy on the eyes for late-night cooking planning.
